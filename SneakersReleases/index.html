The Sneaker of the Year: Jordan 1: Lost and Found
Dubbed initially “Chicago Reimagined,” the upcoming Air Jordan 1 in the classic Bulls colorway has gotten a new name and theme in “Lost and Found.” The Air Jordan 1 High “Lost and Found” is a love letter to the days of the mom-and-pop shop and happening upon a shoe in the wild. The shoe takes on a vintage appearance with its worn look; cracked leather accents appear on the white base and black accents, while the red leather overlays come in pristine condition. An aged white midsole and red outsole with a chalky white look to mimic the dustiness of stored sneakers complete the decades-old feel.

- Retail price: 180$
- Assumed resell price: 400-600$
